Node Folder Structure
    node_modules/
    routes/ - thread pool
    controllers/ - to verify whether data is sent or returned properly
    services/ - service workers (db operations or file operations or supports your project)
    .gitignore
    index.js or server.js or express.js (event queue and event loop)
    package.json
    package-lock.json
